Got the Feels?

What is Got the Feels? Got the Feels is an open social network that helps UCSD students struggling with mental health issues connect with a community of their peers for mutual support. Got the Feels was founded in 2016 by a group of five students concerned with the overall state of mental health on UCSD’s campus. United by the goal of improving awareness of mental health issues and access to immediate help for those in need, we started searching for obstacles to these goals. What we found was a lack of access to spaces where students who have struggled can meet, discuss, and support each other through their problems.

Here at Got the Feels, we believe that by uniting students of all walks of life who have experienced these issues on any scale we can create an online “space” for students to vent, support one another, and arrange meetups when access to a professional is not immediately available or necessary. Whether you are experiencing serious grief or mild stress, our loving community supports any participation, just remember to stay encouraging!

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Q: How do I join?

A: Got the Feels is powered by Twitter.com’s login system, to log into our system just use your Twitter username and password. Don’t worry, we never store this information so your passwords stay safe!

Q: What if I don’t have a Twitter account?

A: Our current platform only accepts Twitter login information. If you don’t have Twitter, you can create a basic account at https://twitter.com/signup. All you need is an email address.

Q: How do I create a post?

A: Creating a post is easy! From the home page, simply enter one of the chat rooms that describes your emotions best, and start typing. You post will be immediately viewable by others in the room who understand what you are going through.

Q: How can I privately message other users?

A: Our topic based chat-rooms are designed to be an open forum for people to share their feelings. If you make a connection with another user, you can enter the “meetups” room, where you can privately message other users who want to meet up in person or online via Twitter’s messaging system.

Q: How can I find more professional help?

A: Got the Feels is designed to be a supportive community of peers, however we recognize that this support can only go so far. If you feel like you need more serious help, please reach out to the UCSD Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) at 858-534-3755 to schedule an appointment. If you are having a psychological emergency and are thinking about harming yourself, please contact 911.
